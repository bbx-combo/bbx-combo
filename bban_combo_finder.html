BBAN Combo Finder

Filter Beyblade X combos by Blade, Ratchet, or Bit and rank them by accumulated podium points. Lockchips for CX Beys are either listed as "metal" or "plastic", and combos will list assist blades: Metal Blast Heavy and Plastic Blast Wheel.
Data note: This page is based on a small Beyblade Australia Network (BBAN) dataset dating back to September 2025. Points are cumulative from recorded results: 1st place = 3 points, 2nd place = 2 points, 3rd place = 1 point, and other noted appearances = 0.1 points. This version also adds the cleaned BBAN non-podium appearance list as additional 0.1-point entries.
